Our Recipes

Butter Chicken


Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani, originating in New Delhi. It is a type of curry made from chicken with spiced tomato and butter sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture.

Dhokla


Dhokla is a savoury sponge dish native to the state of Gujarat and parts of adjacent states, and is popular throughout the country. It is made with a fermented batter that is steamed to a cake-like consistency.


Idli Sambhar


Idli is type of savoury rice cake, originating from South India, popular as breakfast food in Southern India and in Sri Lanka. The fermentation process breaks down starches so that they are more readily metabolised by body.
